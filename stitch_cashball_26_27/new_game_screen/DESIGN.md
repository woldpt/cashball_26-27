# Deep Forest Technical Design System

## Visual Identity & Tone
This design system embodies a "Technical Noir" aesthetic, blending organic deep-forest tones with a precise, industrial layout. It is designed for high-performance interfaces that require a sophisticated dark environment with high-contrast functional elements.

## Color Palette
The palette is rooted in deep botanical greens and heavy charcoal, accented by a prestigious gold.
*   **Primary (#2D6A4F):** A lush, dense green used for core interactions and primary brand signals.
*   **Secondary (#4A4A4A):** A muted slate that provides structural depth without competing with the primary green.
*   **Tertiary (#D4AF37):** A metallic gold accent for specialized status indicators, badges, and premium highlights.
*   **Neutral (#121212):** A deep black-base for background surfaces, ensuring optimal readability in dark mode.

## Typography
The system utilizes a hybrid approach between geometric modernism and functional clarity.
*   **Headlines (Space Grotesk):** A technical typeface with unique character shapes that provides a futuristic, precise feel.
*   **Body & Labels (Inter):** A workhorse sans-serif designed for maximum legibility at small sizes, ensuring data and content remain clear.

## Shape and Structure
*   **Roundedness (1 - Subtle):** Corners feature a slight 4px radius, softening the industrial edge while maintaining a professional, structured appearance.
*   **Spacing (2 - Normal):** The layout utilizes a standard, balanced density. This ensures comfortable breathing room between components, moving away from a cramped technical interface to a more readable, professional environment.

## Design Principles
1.  **Precision through Geometry:** Utilize the angular nature of Space Grotesk and subtle rounding to convey reliability.
2.  **Organic Authority:** Lean into the deep greens to create an environment that feels grounded and premium.
3.  **Balanced Density:** Maintain a standard spacing rhythm to ensure complex data-heavy screens remain scannable and unintimidating.
